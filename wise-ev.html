The network

A hub-and-spoke model — built for the way Americans actually drive.

Hubs cluster high-power chargers near major corridors and dense commercial areas. Spokes extend the network into retail, municipal, and workplace locations — so a charger is always on the way.

HUB

Hubs for power. Spokes for reach.

Each hub is a destination — solar canopy, BESS-backed peak shaving, NanoWise-protected fire suppression, retail partner on site. Spokes are partner locations (retail, municipal, fleet depots) that extend density without diluting margin.

CBRE manages site acquisition and installation. WISE Power installs the BESS layer. Every hub doubles as a showcase for the rest of the WEG ecosystem.
