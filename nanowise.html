The technology

Three properties that change the math on lithium fires.

Conventional suppression was built for hydrocarbon and electrical fires. Lithium thermal runaway propagates differently — through chain reactions inside the cells themselves. NanoWise targets the mechanism, not the symptom.

PROP / 01

Penetrates the cell stack

Nano-scale particles infiltrate gaps in cell packs that conventional agents cannot reach — interrupting propagation before adjacent cells go critical.

PROP / 02

Endothermic absorption

The formulation pulls thermal energy out of the failure zone at the surface area scale enabled by nanoparticles — orders of magnitude beyond bulk agents.

PROP / 03

Drop-in for existing systems

Designed to integrate with standard suppression infrastructure. Engineering retrofit, not a rebuild.
