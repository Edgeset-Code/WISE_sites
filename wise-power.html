How we size a project

Every project is different. We engineer solutions that fit — not the other way around.

01

Understand your needs

Facility size, load profile, hours of operation, and goals. Where are you paying peak demand charges?

02

Assess the site

Our engineers evaluate your location, existing electrical infrastructure, and available space.

03

Design the solution

Custom system — one container or many — placed exactly where it's needed. Modeled against your bills.

04

Install & commission

End-to-end deployment, typically within days. We manage the full process.

Real example. Sporting KC's Children's Mercy Park needed 3 separate containers — not one — because of how meter rooms were distributed through the stadium. Result: $600K saved annually.
