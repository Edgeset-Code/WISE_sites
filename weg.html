Who we are

One parent company. Three market-facing divisions. One unified ecosystem.

WEG began as WillCo Technologies — a Kansas City IT firm that grew a cybersecurity program to 1.6 million registered DoD users. In 2016 our founder was awarded patents for a Distributed Energy Source System, launching WISE Power. Today WEG spans solar, storage, EV charging, and nanotechnology — with active projects in the US, India, and Africa.
